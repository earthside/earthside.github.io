Shared Ownership at EarthSide Retreat

Welcome to a Partnership with Nature

At EarthSide Retreat, we believe in creating partnerships that are as enduring and beautiful as the majestic vistas of Mahabaleshwar itself. Shared ownership at EarthSide Retreat isn't just about having a stake in property; it's about embracing a lifestyle that intertwines serenity with luxury, while being deeply rooted in nature's splendor.

How Shared Ownership Works

Imagine owning a piece of paradise where every visit feels like coming home, yet without the hassle that typically comes with property ownership. At EarthSide Retreat, shared ownership means you acquire a percentage of the owning company that holds the asset: our boutique resort.

Investing in shared ownership is straightforward:

  1. Choose Your Share: Depending on your lifestyle and investment capacity, select the amount of share you wish to own in the company that is directly tied to the physical asset of EarthSide Retreat.
  2. Legal Formalities: Complete the necessary legal documentation to formalize your stake, guaranteeing your ownership percentage and your rights.
  3. Enjoy the Benefits: Revel in the investor privileges that come with your share, including stay credits and a voice in the community of co-owners.

Benefits of Shared Ownership

Here’s why becoming a shared owner at EarthSide Retreat is a decision that benefits you on multiple fronts:

  1. Asset-Backed Investment: Your investment is tangible and solid, secured by the land and the boutique resort that stands on it. As the value of the asset grows, so does the worth of your investment.
  2. Hassle-Free Ownership: With shared ownership, leave the managerial aspects to us. Expert teams maintain and run the resort to the highest standards, ensuring that your investment is in capable hands while you relax.
  3. Exclusive Access: Enjoy exclusive stay periods at EarthSide Retreat, experiencing firsthand the luxury and tranquility that you are a part owner of. Use your stay credits to spend time in a place that feels like your own.
  4. Potential Earnings: As a shared owner, you may receive a share of the profits from the resort's operations. Your investment can potentially pay dividends beyond the serene experiences.
  5. Legacy Investment: Shared ownership at EarthSide Retreat isn't just for the present; it's a legacy you can pass down to future generations, ensuring they have a personal escape that values both luxury and nature's touch.

Your Retreat, Our Commitment

At EarthSide Retreat, we are devoted to an ethos of sustainable luxury and shared prosperity. By becoming a shared owner, you join a community that values impeccable service, spectacular surroundings, and an investment that grows with the land it celebrates.

Take your first step towards a lifelong journey of leisure, tranquility, and smart investing with EarthSide Retreat's shared ownership.

Paradise Hotel Faq

Frequently Questions

Contact Us

How is the fixed rental income calculated in the Investor and Leisure Plans?

The fixed rental income in both the Investor and Leisure Plans is calculated as a percentage of your total investment amount. This percentage is predetermined and ensures a steady and predictable income stream throughout the investment period. The specific percentage details can be found in the plan documentation, providing clarity on the returns you can expect from your investment.

How is the room points system calculated, and can I choose any type of room with my points?

The room points system is calculated based on your investment plan, with each plan offering a specific allocation of points. These points provide added flexibility, allowing you to choose from a variety of room types based on availability and your preferences. The allocation details are outlined in the plan documentation, ensuring transparency and giving you the freedom to tailor your stay experience to your liking.

What happens if I don't make any referrals or if I make more than 10 referrals?

There is no limit to the number of referrals you can make in our investment plans. If you don't make any referrals, your core investment returns, including fixed rental income and room points, will still be applicable based on your chosen plan.

If you make more than 10 referrals, you will continue to receive referral benefits for each additional referral. There is no cap on the number of referrals, allowing you to maximize your benefits through successful referrals.

Can I sell my investment or transfer it to someone else Can I sell my investment or transfer it to someone else?

Yes, you have the flexibility to sell or transfer your investment to another interested party. This process involves a simple and transparent transfer mechanism facilitated by our team. Whether you choose to sell your share or transfer it to a family member, friend, or another investor, our support team will guide you through the seamless process, ensuring a smooth transition of ownership. However, it is essential to comply with membership rules, company laws, and regulations during the transfer process.

What happens if the construction is delayed?

In the rare event of construction delays, rest assured that your investment and benefits are safeguarded. Earthside Retreats is committed to transparent communication and will keep you informed about any delays. Your fixed rental income and other benefits will commence as per the agreed-upon terms, ensuring that you receive the full value of your investment once the resort is ready for occupancy.

Additionally, there is an opportunity to start the resort partially, one phase at a time. The payment structure is construction-linked, providing further protection for investors from delays. This approach ensures that your investment is closely tied to the progress of the construction, minimizing any potential risks associated with project timelines. We prioritize delivering a high-quality and timely project, and any unforeseen delays will be addressed promptly with the utmost transparency.

What is the process to book a room using my points in the Leisure Plan and the Entrepreneur Plan?

Booking a room using your points in the Leisure Plan and the Entrepreneur Plan is a simple and user-friendly process. Once you decide to redeem your accumulated room points, you can log in to our dedicated member portal on our website. Within the portal, you will find an easy-to-navigate booking interface where you can select your preferred dates, room type, and other preferences.

For the Leisure Plan, you can use your room points to enjoy a stay at the resort, offering you flexibility and choice in your vacation experience.

For the Entrepreneur Plan, not only can you book a room for your own stay, but you also have the option to use your points to book rooms for guests (your customer). This flexibility allows you to share the luxury experience with friends, family, or even offer it as a special gift.

Our goal is to make the redemption process seamless and enjoyable, ensuring that you make the most of your accumulated room points and experience the luxury of Earthside Retreats.

What happens to my investment if the resort goes bankrupt?

In the unlikely event of the resort facing bankruptcy, your investment is protected through a robust legal and financial structure. Earthside Retreats operates under a company structure, and your ownership shares are safeguarded by relevant company laws and regulations. The resort's financial health is managed independently of individual investments, providing a layer of security for our investors. Additionally, the shared ownership model ensures that no single investor bears the entire financial burden, minimizing risks associated with unforeseen circumstances. Rest assured, we prioritize transparency and financial responsibility to protect the interests of our valued investors.

In the event of a dispute, how is it resolved? Is there a designated arbitration process?

In the event of a dispute, Earthside Retreats has a designated arbitration process in place to ensure fair and transparent resolution. Our membership and investment agreements include provisions for arbitration, and a neutral third-party arbitrator or arbitration service is engaged to facilitate the resolution process. This approach aims to provide an impartial and efficient mechanism for addressing any disagreements or conflicts that may arise. We are committed to fostering open communication and finding equitable solutions, and the arbitration process is designed to uphold the interests of all parties involved.

What are the resort's occupancy rates? How does this affect my potential returns?

The occupancy rate of the resort will depend on a variety of factors, such as the location, amenities, and marketing efforts. We anticipate that the resort will have a high occupancy rate due to its prime location and unique amenities. This will result in higher returns for investors, as the resort will be able to generate more revenue from guests.Returns will be according to membership plan for the first 3 years. Beyond 3 years, it will be actual.

How does Earthside plan to attract guests to the resort to ensure high occupancy rates?

Earthside plans to attract guests to the resort by leveraging a comprehensive marketing strategy. This includes creating a strong brand identity for the resort, identifying the target audience, highlighting the key features and benefits of the resort, offering packages and special offers, and including information about local attractions and activities. We also plan to include guest testimonials and reviews to build credibility and trust with potential guests. Additionally, we plan to leverage digital marketing channels such as social media, search engine optimization, and email marketing to reach a wider audience. We will also tie up with popular travel agencies, corporates and travel portals.

What happens if I don't use my room points within a given year? Do they carry over?

Certainly, to ensure a balance of utilization and flexibility, Earthside Retreats has a policy that allows a certain percentage of your accumulated room points to be carried over to the next year. This policy is designed to encourage our members to enjoy the benefits of their membership regularly while still providing some flexibility for those special occasions or unexpected changes in plans. The specific percentage and details regarding the carry-over limits can be found in our membership guidelines. Our aim is to strike the right balance between flexibility and active engagement for a rewarding experience at Earthside Retreats.
