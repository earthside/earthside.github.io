What is the "Emergency and Corpus Fund" used for?

The Emergency and Corpus Fund is a fund set aside by the company to cover any unexpected costs or expenses that may arise during the course of the project. This fund is used to cover any unforeseen costs or expenses that may arise during the construction or operation of the resort. This fund is also used to cover any legal or regulatory costs that may arise during the course of the project. It is also used to undertake any major repair needed to upkeep the resort.

How is the fixed rental income calculated in the Investor and Leisure Plans?

The fixed rental income is calculated as a percentage of the total investment amount. For the Investor Plan, the fixed rental income is 9.3% of the total investment amount, and for the Leisure Plan, the fixed rental income is 4% of the total investment amount.

What happens if I don't make any referrals or if I make more than 10 referrals?

For the purpose of calculation, We are assuming that customers will be able to provide 10 referrals per year. Referral payout are directly proportional to numbers of referral. If you make more than 10 referrals, you will be eligible for additional referral benefits at the same rate.If you don't make any referrals, you will still be eligible for the fixed rental income and resort stay benefit.

How is the room points system calculated, and can I choose any type of room with my points?

The room points system is based on the type of room and the season. For example, a 2 BHK room in peak season will require 200 points, while a 2 Bed Loft Cottage in peak season will require 125 points. You can choose any type of room with your points, depending on the availability.
